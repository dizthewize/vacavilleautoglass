### /services/windshield-repair

**H1:** Windshield Chip & Crack Repair in Vacaville

**Benefit statement:** Most chips repaired in 30 minutes. Insurance usually waives the deductible.

**Copy:**
A rock chip the size of a quarter can turn into a 12-inch crack overnight — a pothole, a hot-and-cold cycle, or a door slam is often enough. Repairing the chip early preserves the original factory seal of your windshield (which is always stronger than a replacement) and typically costs nothing out-of-pocket under California's no-deductible chip repair provisions.

Our technician injects optical-grade resin into the chip under vacuum, cures it with UV light, and polishes it flush. The repair restores 90–95% of structural integrity and is barely visible. Total time: usually under 30 minutes.

**Benefits:**
- Saves your original factory windshield
- Often free with comprehensive insurance
- 30 minutes at your location
- Prevents chip from spreading into a full crack

**CTA:** **Book a Free Chip Repair**

### /services/adas-calibration

**H1:** ADAS Calibration After Windshield Replacement

**Benefit statement:** Factory-certified recalibration for lane-assist, collision warning, and adaptive cruise.

**Copy:**
Modern vehicles use a camera mounted to the windshield for lane departure, collision warning, adaptive cruise control, traffic-sign recognition, and automatic high-beams. Every time that windshield is replaced, the camera's alignment shifts by fractions of a degree — enough to miscalibrate the entire ADAS suite.

We perform both static (shop target board) and dynamic (road-test) ADAS calibration to OEM specifications. A calibration certificate is provided with every job — required by most insurance policies and dealer warranties.

**Benefits:**
- Static + dynamic calibration as required by manufacturer
- All major OEMs supported (Honda, Toyota, Ford, GM, Subaru, Tesla, etc.)
- Calibration certificate provided
- Performed on-site after windshield installation

**CTA:** **Ask About ADAS Calibration**
