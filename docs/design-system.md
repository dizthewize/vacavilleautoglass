| `--color-text-inverse` on `--color-accent` | 3.2:1 | ✓ PASS AA — large text (≥18px bold) |

**Rule:** CTA buttons on `--color-accent` background use `--color-text-inverse` (white) across all button sizes. Primary-CTA Archivo weights (700/800/900) satisfy WCAG AA large-text criteria at every size in the scale. White-on-orange is the committed, system-consistent treatment — it lands the "Industrial Trust" voice more authoritatively than dark-on-orange and matches the industry-standard auto-service CTA pattern.

**Font pairings:**
- **Archivo** (display) — A geometric sans with broad apertures and strong weight variation. Used at weights 700 / 800 / 900 for headlines. Reads confident, industrial, automotive — exactly the voice for a trades-focused service brand. Explicitly avoids the "Inter everywhere" generic-AI aesthetic.
- **Manrope** (body) — A modern humanist sans with warmth. Used at weights 400 / 500 / 600 / 700 for body copy, UI, and form labels. Readable at small sizes, pairs well with Archivo's stronger display voice, and has a friendly personality that offsets Archivo's authority.

## Aesthetic Direction

This design commits to **"Industrial Trust"** — a practical, confident, blue-collar-authority aesthetic built for a mobile auto glass service business serving Solano County. Think premium local auto shop signage translated to the web: strong typography, generous whitespace, real photography of real work, and a color system that signals automotive expertise (deep navy) combined with visible urgency (safety orange). No glossy tech-startup polish, no playful illustrations, no skeuomorphism. The site should feel like it was made by someone who replaces windshields for a living — and is proud of the work.

**Border-radius convention:** medium (6–10px) on cards, buttons, inputs. Never fully rounded pills for primary CTAs (that reads "SaaS"), never sharp 0px corners (too brutalist for a customer-facing trades site).

**Shadow usage:** subtle throughout (`--shadow-sm` / `--shadow-md`). One exception: the primary CTA button gets an orange-tinted glow (`--shadow-cta`) to draw the eye without resorting to gradient shine.

**Motion posture:** moderate — hover states on buttons and cards (150ms), one hero entrance fade-in from below on initial load, and smooth accordion open/close for FAQs. No scroll-triggered parallax, no staggered reveals on service grids, no decorative animation. The site must feel fast and serious.

**Layout dominant pattern:** full-bleed hero + boxed content (`max-width: 1200px`) for all section content. Generous negative space between sections (`--section-py: 5rem`). Asymmetry used sparingly — notably in the hero, which uses a 2-column split on desktop (copy left, lead form right) rather than a centered hero.

**Imagery style:** photorealistic only. Real windshields, real cracks under sunlight, real technicians in Vacaville Auto Glass branded shirts working on real cars. Absolutely no staged stock photos of smiling people in business casual in front of generic cars. A windshield with a rock chip on a Ford F-150 at a Vacaville Starbucks parking lot is better than a polished studio shot every time.

### Anti-patterns to avoid (do-not-do list)

1. **No purple gradients** — wrong industry signal. This is a trades service, not a SaaS product.
2. **No Inter or Roboto** — generic AI aesthetic. Archivo + Manrope commit to a distinct voice.
3. **No glassmorphism or frosted overlays** — feels airy/premium when this brand needs grounded/serious.
4. **No card-grid-on-card-grid layouts** — services grid is a single card row on its own section background; don't nest.
5. **No emoji icons as primary illustration** — line icons or photography only. Emoji acceptable only for trust-signal checkmarks or phone/clock inline glyphs.
6. **No "lorem ipsum" or generic stock headlines** — every copy block is grounded in auto glass industry specifics from `docs/site-plan.md`.
7. **No fully rounded pills for primary CTAs** — reads SaaS. Use `--radius-md` (6px) on primary buttons.

### Conversion

#### Button

All buttons use Archivo 700 (primary) or 600 (secondary), letter-spacing `--ls-wide`, border-radius `--radius-md`.

- **Primary** — CTAs for lead capture and "Get a Quote"
  - Background: `--color-accent` (safety orange)
  - Text: `--color-text-inverse` (white) — at Archivo 700/800/900 weights, meets WCAG AA large-text contrast (3.2:1 at ≥18px bold)
  - Hover: `--color-accent-dark` bg, `transform: translateY(-1px)`, `--shadow-cta`
  - Focus: `--focus-ring`
  - Active: `transform: translateY(0)`, reduce shadow
  - Works identically on light and dark surfaces — no separate inverse variant needed

- **Secondary** — secondary actions, "Learn More"
  - Background: transparent; Border: 2px solid `--color-primary`
  - Text: `--color-primary`
  - Hover: `--color-primary` bg, `--color-text-inverse` text

- **Ghost** — low-friction links inside dense sections
  - No background, no border
  - Text: `--color-primary` with bottom-only underline
  - Hover: removes underline, adds `--color-accent` text

- **Sizes:**
  - sm: `--text-sm`, padding `--space-2` `--space-4`
  - md (default): `--text-base`, padding `--space-3` `--space-6`
  - lg: `--text-lg`, padding `--space-4` `--space-8`

#### LeadCaptureForm

Fields (in order):
1. **Name** (`type="text"`, required, autocomplete="name")
2. **Phone** (`type="tel"`, required, autocomplete="tel", pattern-validated, placeholder "(916) 555-0100")
3. **Vehicle Year/Make/Model** (`type="text"`, required, placeholder "2021 Toyota Camry")
4. **Service Needed** (`select`) — options: "Windshield Replacement", "Windshield Chip/Crack Repair", "Side Window", "Rear Window", "ADAS Calibration", "Not Sure — Help Me Diagnose"
5. **ZIP / City** (`type="text"`, required, placeholder "95687")
6. **Email** (`type="email"`, optional, autocomplete="email")
7. **Notes/Photo of damage** (`textarea`, optional) — note: photo upload deferred to contact page form
8. **Honeypot** (`type="text"`, name="website", `tabindex="-1"`, visually hidden)

Submit button: primary, full-width, lg size. Text: "**Get My Free Quote**"
Trust micro-copy below: "Licensed & Insured · Mobile Service · All Insurance Accepted"

Label style: Manrope 600, `--text-sm`, `--color-text`, margin-bottom `--space-2`
Input style: `--text-base`, padding `--space-3` `--space-4`, border `1px solid var(--color-border-strong)`, border-radius `--radius-md`, background `--color-bg`
Focus state: border `--color-accent`, `--focus-ring`

Error state: border `--color-error`, helper text below in `--color-error` at `--text-sm`.

## Accessibility Checklist

- [ ] Color contrast: all text/bg combos verified against WCAG AA (see Contrast Ratios table above)
- [ ] Touch targets: all interactive elements ≥ 44×44px (MobileStickyBar buttons 64px tall)
- [ ] Focus indicators: visible 3px `--color-accent` ring on all focusable elements
- [ ] Phone links: all phone references use `<a href="tel:+19166122126">` with `aria-label="Call Vacaville Auto Glass"`
- [ ] Form labels: every form field has an associated `<label>` (not just placeholder)
- [ ] Form errors: field-level errors with `aria-describedby` linking to helper text
- [ ] Images: all `<img>` have descriptive `alt` attributes (see Image Brief)
- [ ] Decorative images: marked `alt=""` and `role="presentation"`
- [ ] Accordion: `aria-expanded`, `aria-controls`, `role="region"` on panels; keyboard navigation (Enter/Space/ArrowUp/ArrowDown)
- [ ] Mobile sticky bar: hidden on desktop (`@media (min-width: 768px) { display: none }`); not announced to screen readers when hidden
- [ ] Skip-to-content link: first focusable element on every page, visible on focus
- [ ] Heading hierarchy: one H1 per page, no skipped levels (H2 → H3 → H4, not H2 → H4)
- [ ] Language: `<html lang="en">`
- [ ] Reduced motion: `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` disables all entrance animations and hover translates
- [ ] Forced colors: components tested against `forced-colors: active` (Windows high-contrast mode) — borders remain visible, focus rings preserved
